Strength-first home training for body composition.

This interactive plan combines a 5-minute dynamic warmup with a 20-minute circuit built around push-ups, lunges, squats, planks, short-burst conditioning, plus dumbbells and bands for progressive overload.

Use it as a repeatable full-body session, log your results weekly, and increase reps, load, band tension, tempo, or density once performance becomes consistent.

Dynamic prep

5-minute warmup

Move continuously. Aim to raise temperature, open the hips and shoulders, and prepare your trunk for planks and loaded patterns.

  • 1 minute: March in place with band pull-aparts.
  • 1 minute: Goblet squat with a light dumbbell, controlled tempo.
  • 1 minute: Alternating reverse lunge with overhead reach or light band tension.
  • 1 minute: Walk-out to high plank, then stand and perform 4-6 band rows.
  • 1 minute: Easy rope skip, fast march, or band-resisted march.

Warmup cues

  • Keep the first minute easy and rhythmic rather than explosive.
  • Use a light load only; the goal is activation, not fatigue.
  • On the walk-out, brace your ribs down before stepping into plank.
  • If skipping feels awkward, swap for a fast march or low pogo hops.
Main session

20-minute circuit

Complete 3 rounds. Rest 20-30 seconds between exercises and 60-90 seconds between rounds.

Exercise Category Suggested reps / time Rest Load ideas Form cue
Push-ups (band-resisted or incline if needed) Strength 8-12 reps 20-30 s Band over upper back; incline on bench to scale Keep ribs down and reach chest fully between hands.
Goblet squat Strength 10-15 reps 20-30 s 1 dumbbell at chest; add tempo before heavier load Sit between the hips, stay tall, drive evenly through feet.
Forearm plank Core 25-40 s 20-30 s Band around upper arms or bodyweight Glutes tight, chin tucked lightly, no sagging hips.
Reverse lunges Strength 8-12 / leg 20-30 s Dumbbells at sides or goblet hold Step back long enough to keep front foot planted and stable.
Push-ups variation 2 Strength 6-10 reps 20-30 s Narrow grip, tempo push-up, or band assistance Lower under control for 3 seconds before pressing hard.
High-plank shoulder taps Core 10-16 / side 20-30 s Band around wrists for added tension Widen the feet and keep hips as quiet as possible.
Rope sprint or band high knees Burst 20-30 s 30-40 s Rope sprint, band-resisted high knees, or DB thrusters Stay powerful, crisp, and stop before technique breaks.
Glute bridge / hip thrust Strength 12-20 reps 20-30 s Dumbbell on hips or loop band over hips Pause at the top for 1-2 seconds and keep ribs stacked.
Side plank Core 20-30 s / side 20-30 s Bodyweight, light DB on top hip, or mini-band around thighs Press the floor away and keep a straight line from ankle to ear.
